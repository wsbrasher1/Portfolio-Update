About Me

Hello…my name is Johnny Cash William S. Brasher.

Since we’re friends now, you can just call me Bill.

I’m a fledgling full-stack web developer, published author, voice over talent and 20+ year veteran of the advertising industry. I’ve worked tirelessly over the years to help leading companies drive sales growth while maintaining an excellent brand image. In short, I’m a problem solver at heart and I’m passionate about helping others create visually appealing, user-friendly and innovative web sites.

When I’m not saving the world (one site at a time), I’m usually spending time outdoors…camping, hiking or boating with my dog, Lyla, in tow. I’m a fan of great design and how it impacts our lives on a daily basis in everything from art to architecture and, yes, even web sites.

Don’t be a stranger – I’d love to hear about your next project and how I can help you succeed.
